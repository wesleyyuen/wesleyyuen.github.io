Combat

The Combat system revolves around swordplay, specifically a wakizashi that can be thrown and recalled. Throw your wakizashi at enemies at a distance and recall to pull the enemy towards yourself!

But beware! Throwing away your weapon may leave you vulnerable!

Movement

Smooth movement is essential to engage players in exploration. Many movement mechanics like varying jump heights, coyote time, jump buffering are implemented to ensure responsive player control!

A further mechanics is planned to be implemented: By throwing and lodging the weapon onto a wall, player can use it as an additional platform to traverse the map! Combine this with dashes to perform satisfying momvements!
